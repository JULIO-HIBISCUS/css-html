ABOUT ROSELLE HIBISCUS

Roselle has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for various health purposes. Some potential health benefits include: Rich in Vitamin C: Roselle is a good source of vitamin C, which helps boost the immune system and promotes healthy skin. Antioxidant Properties: It is packed with antioxidants, which may help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the body. Blood Pressure Regulation: Some studies suggest that roselle tea may help lower blood pressure, particularly in people with mild to moderate hypertension. Digestive Health: It can act as a mild laxative, promoting digestive health and easing constipation. Anti-inflammatory Effects: Roselle has compounds that may help reduce inflammation in the body, potentially helping with conditions like arthritis.
